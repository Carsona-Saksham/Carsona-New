Cancellations and Refunds

Understanding our return, exchange, and refund policies

Order Cancellation Policy

Before Shipping
  • Free Cancellation: Up to 1 hours after order placement
  • Cancellation after 1 hour of order placement: 50% of the bill for cancellations after 1 hour but before shipping
  • Cancellation after shipping: 100% of the bill for cancellations after shipping
  • Full Refund: Processed within 5-7 business days
After Shipping
  • Cannot Cancel: Once shipped, order cannot be cancelled

Refund Policy

Refund Methods
  • Original Payment Method: Credit/Debit cards, Digital wallets
  • Bank Transfer: For cash on delivery orders
  • Store Credit: Optional for faster processing
Processing Times
  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-10 business days

Product Price100% Refund

Exchange Policy

Free Exchanges

We offer free exchanges for size/fit issues or if you receive the wrong product. Exchange requests must be made within 15 days of delivery.

Exchange Conditions
  • Product must be unused and in original packaging
  • Exchange for same or higher value product
  • Price difference to be paid for higher value items
  • Same model/color subject to availability
Exchange Process
  • Contact customer service for exchange request
  • We arrange pickup of original product
  • New product shipped after quality check
  • No additional shipping charges

Special Cases

Defective Products
  • Immediate Replacement: For manufacturing defects
  • Free Return Shipping: We bear all costs
  • Express Processing: Priority handling within 24 hours
  • Compensation: Additional discount on next purchase
Wrong Product Delivered
  • Immediate Exchange: No questions asked
  • Free Pickup & Delivery: We handle all logistics
  • Priority Shipping: Correct product shipped immediately
  • Apology Discount: 10% off next purchase

Installation-Related Returns

Installation Issues

If installation services don't meet your expectations:

  • Free re-installation within 7 days
  • Full refund of installation charges if issue persists
  • Product return option if installation damages the product
  • Compensation for any damage caused during installation
